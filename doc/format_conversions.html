Date Format Converions

Year:
Ruby formatDate Notes
%y y year (two digit)
%Y yy year (four digit)
%C none century number (year divided by 100) 20

Month:
Ruby formatDate Notes
none m month of year (no leading zero)
%m mm month of year (two digit)
%b or %h M month name short
%B MM month name long

Week:
Ruby formatDate Notes
%U none week number of the year, Sunday as first day of week (00-53)
%W none week number of the year, Monday as first day of week (00-53)

Day:
Ruby formatDate Notes
%e d day of month (no leading zero)
%d dd day of month (two digit)
none o day of the year (no leading zeros)
%j oo day of the year (three digit)
%w none weekday as a number, with 0 representing Sunday (0-6)
%u none weekday as a number, with 1 representing Monday (1-7)
%a D day of week name short
%A DD day of week name long

Time:
Ruby formatDate Notes
%H hh hour (24-hour clock) (00-23)
%k h hour (24-hour clock); single digits preceded by space ( 0-23)
%I hh hour (12-hour clock) (01-12)
%l h hour (12-hour clock); single digits preceded by space ( 1-12)
%M mm minute (00-59)
none m minute (0-59)
%S ss seconds (00-59)
none s seconds (0-59)
none t a or p for AM/PM
none tt A or P for AM/PM
none T am or pm for AM/PM
%p TT AM or PM for AM/PM
%Z none timezone name or abbreviation EDT
%z none timezone offset from UTC -0400
